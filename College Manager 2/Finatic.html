Finatic

A youth-run non-profit advancing financial literacy across the nation through engaging workshops and awareness initiatives.

About Us:

Finatic's goal is to educate the next generation of youth on crucial money management skills, fostering the confidence to make correct financial judgments and decisions. Through handcrafted curriculum, interactive workshops, engaging informational videos, newsletters and social media we hope to provide accessible, informative financial literacy knowledge and raise awareness about financial illiteracy, an issue highlighted by COVID-19.

We partner with local schools, community centers, and libraries to bring interactive and informative workshops that teach financial literacy basics.

Every other month we host online events that involve fun challenges, guest speakers, and mini-competitions about financial literacy and the importance of money management.
